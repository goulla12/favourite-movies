Baahubali: The Beginning

Year: 2015

Director: S. S. Rajamouli

Cast: Prabhas, Rana Daggubati, Anushka Shetty, Tamannaah

Genre: Action, Adventure, Drama

Synopsis: The epic story of Baahubali is set in the ancient kingdom of Mahishmati, where two brothers fight for the throne. The movie explores themes of loyalty, betrayal, and vengeance with grand visuals and action sequences.
