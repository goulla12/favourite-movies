Magadheera

Year: 2009

Director: S. S. Rajamouli

Cast: Ram Charan, Kajal Aggarwal

Genre: Action, Fantasy, Romance

Synopsis: A story that transcends centuries, Magadheera tells the tale of a warrior from the past who reincarnates to take revenge on his enemies. The movie features stunning action sequences, visual effects, and a beautiful love story.
